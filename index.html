Our Story

More Than Coffee — It's a Feeling

Accaza Coffee was born from a simple belief: that a great cup of coffee has the power to slow down time, spark conversations, and make any moment feel a little more special.

Nestled in the heart of Dasmariñas City, we source the finest beans, roast with care, and brew with passion — all so you can sit back, breathe, and enjoy the moment.

Accaza Coffee House is a hidden gem right along the roadside near SM Dasmariñas — easy to find whether you're commuting or driving. Inside, it's surprisingly spacious with a calm, serene atmosphere that's rare among today's cramped cafés.

The coffee is outstanding, with well-crafted flavors from bold to smooth. But what truly sets Accaza apart is how perfectly it serves both students and professionals — it's a productive sanctuary where you can focus, study, or work in peace. Comfortable seating, peaceful ambiance, and a space that lets hours pass without distraction.

Highly recommended for anyone looking for great coffee and a place to get things done. ☕✨
