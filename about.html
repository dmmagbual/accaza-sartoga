Our Story

Brewed With Passion

A hidden gem along Governor's Drive — surprisingly spacious, calm and welcoming.

Nestled in the heart of Dasmariñas City, Accaza Coffee House sources the finest beans, roasts with care, and brews with passion. From the roadside near SM Dasmariñas you might not expect it — but step inside and you'll find a surprisingly spacious, serene space that feels worlds away from the bustle outside.

We built Accaza to be a productive sanctuary — a place where students, professionals and friends can focus, study, work, or simply slow down over a well-crafted cup. Every drink, from our signature Sea Salt Caramel series to a classic Spanish Latte, is made with genuine care.
