Our services

Hydraulic Cylinder Service

Service photo
assets/img/service-1.png … service-6.png

We fully strip down your hydraulic cylinder and assess every component for wear, damage and tolerance loss. Our technicians document the condition of the barrel, rod, seals and end caps so you get a clear, honest picture of what needs attention before any work begins.

We restore worn, scored or out-of-round cylinder barrels to factory specification using honing and re-boring. This brings the bore back to a precise finish and diameter, protecting your seals and ensuring smooth, leak-free operation.

Bent, pitted or corroded piston rods are replaced with precision-machined rods built to match your original specifications. We use high-grade materials and exact tolerances to restore full strength and a flawless sealing surface.

We apply a durable, corrosion-resistant hard chrome layer to rods and other critical surfaces. This extends component life, reduces friction and provides excellent protection against wear in demanding operating environments.

Our certified welders repair cracks, mounting points and structural damage on cylinder bodies and components. Every weld is carried out to industry standards to guarantee strength, integrity and long-term reliability.

Before any unit leaves us, it undergoes full pressure testing to verify performance and confirm there are no leaks. This final quality check ensures your cylinder operates safely and to specification under real working loads.
